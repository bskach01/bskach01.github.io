Although born in Pennsylvania, Ben grew up in Portland, Oregon. He attended Lincoln High School, where he competed on the Constitution Team, was a co-president of the Model United Nations club, ran track & field, and was a leader in the school concert & marching band. He went on to enroll in Tufts University, and recently graduated with a B.A. in Political Science and a mathematics minor. During his time at Tufts, Ben also developed an interest in computer science, taking the opportunity to learn C++, web design, and data structures. Ben hopes to incorporate his interests by utilizing technology to go into a career in communications and social media development.

Throughout his childhood, Ben developed an appreciation for the outdoors. He has now been able to incorporate that passion into his academic and career pursuits. In college, he sought out courses within his major that related to the outdoors and environment, such as the Politics of Oil and Energy and Environmental Policy. In 2015, Ben had the opportunity to intern for the American Hiking Society, where he worked on development and communications. In 2016, he began leading trips for Overland, a company that offers outdoor adventures for elementary through high school students. This past summer, he had the opportunity to lead Overland trips in Colorado. In his free time, Ben enjoys the outdoors through rock climbing, hiking, biking, backpacking, and photography.
